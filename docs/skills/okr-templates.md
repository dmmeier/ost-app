# OKR Output Templates

Use the appropriate template based on the mode. Adapt the structure as needed — these are starting points, not straitjackets. The key is that every output is clean, scannable, and useful to someone who wasn't in the room when the OKRs were written.

---

## Define Template

```markdown
# [Team Name] OKRs — [Quarter, e.g. Q2 2026]

**Cycle:** [Start date] – [End date]
**Team:** [Team name and brief description]
**Methodology:** [Classic / Outcome-based / Hybrid]

---

## Company alignment

> Briefly state the company-level objectives this team's OKRs support.
> If company OKRs aren't available, note the strategic themes guiding this quarter.

---

## Objective 1: [Objective title]

> [One-sentence description of why this matters]

| # | Key Result | Baseline | Target | Owner |
|---|-----------|----------|--------|-------|
| 1.1 | [KR description] | [Current state] | [Target state] | [Name/Role] |
| 1.2 | [KR description] | [Current state] | [Target state] | [Name/Role] |
| 1.3 | [KR description] | [Current state] | [Target state] | [Name/Role] |

**Aligns with:** [Company objective, if applicable]

---

## Objective 2: [Objective title]

> [One-sentence description of why this matters]

| # | Key Result | Baseline | Target | Owner |
|---|-----------|----------|--------|-------|
| 2.1 | [KR description] | [Current state] | [Target state] | [Name/Role] |
| 2.2 | [KR description] | [Current state] | [Target state] | [Name/Role] |

**Aligns with:** [Company objective, if applicable]

---

## Objective 3: [Objective title]

> [Repeat pattern as above]

---

## Summary

| Objective | # of KRs | Primary owner | Company alignment |
|-----------|----------|---------------|-------------------|
| [Obj 1 title] | [N] | [Name] | [Company Obj] |
| [Obj 2 title] | [N] | [Name] | [Company Obj] |
| [Obj 3 title] | [N] | [Name] | [Company Obj] |
```

## Check-in Template

```markdown
# [Team Name] OKR Check-in — [Date]

**Quarter:** [Q and year]
**Week:** [X of 13] ([percentage]% through the quarter)

---

## Overall status

[One-paragraph summary: are we on track overall? Any major shifts in context?]

---

## Objective 1: [Title]

**Overall status:** [On track / At risk / Off track]

| # | Key Result | Target | Current | Progress | Status |
|---|-----------|--------|---------|----------|--------|
| 1.1 | [KR] | [Target] | [Current value] | [X%] | [On track / At risk / Off track] |
| 1.2 | [KR] | [Target] | [Current value] | [X%] | [Status] |

**Notes:** [Brief commentary on what's driving the status, any blockers]

**Actions needed:**
- [Specific action item, if any]

---

## Objective 2: [Title]

[Repeat pattern]

---

## Context changes

[Note any significant changes since OKRs were set — strategy shifts, team changes,
market events, dependency changes — that might warrant adjusting targets or priorities]

## Decisions needed

- [Any decisions the team or leadership needs to make based on this check-in]
```

## Score Template

```markdown
# [Team Name] OKR Scoring — [Quarter, e.g. Q2 2026]

**Cycle:** [Start date] – [End date]
**Scoring method:** [Classic 0–1.0 / Binary / Traffic light / Percentage]

---

## Objective 1: [Title]

**Objective score:** [X.X] / 1.0

| # | Key Result | Target | Actual | Score | Notes |
|---|-----------|--------|--------|-------|-------|
| 1.1 | [KR] | [Target] | [Actual result] | [0.X] | [Brief explanation] |
| 1.2 | [KR] | [Target] | [Actual result] | [0.X] | [Brief explanation] |

**What worked:** [What drove success on this objective]
**What didn't:** [What held us back]
**Carry-forward:** [Anything that should continue into next quarter]

---

## Objective 2: [Title]

[Repeat pattern]

---

## Quarter summary

| Objective | Score | Verdict |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| [Obj 1] | [0.X] | [Met / Partially met / Missed] |
| [Obj 2] | [0.X] | [Verdict] |
| [Obj 3] | [0.X] | [Verdict] |

**Overall quarter score:** [Average or weighted average]

## Key learnings

1. [Learning 1 — what should we do differently next quarter?]
2. [Learning 2]
3. [Learning 3]

## Recommendations for next quarter

- [Recommendation based on this quarter's results]
```

## Alignment Template

```markdown
# OKR Alignment Map — [Quarter]

## Company → Team alignment

| Company Objective | Team Objective | Team | Coverage |
|-------------------|---------------|------|----------|
| [Company Obj 1] | [Team Obj A] | [Team X] | Covered |
| [Company Obj 1] | [Team Obj B] | [Team Y] | Covered |
| [Company Obj 2] | — | — | **Uncovered** |
| [Company Obj 3] | [Team Obj C] | [Team X] | Covered |

### Issues

- **Uncovered company objectives:** [List any company objectives with no team support]
- **Orphan team objectives:** [List any team objectives that don't map to company goals]
- **Overloaded areas:** [Company objectives with too many teams, or teams spread too thin]

---

## Team → Individual alignment (if applicable)

| Team Objective | Individual Objective | Owner | Coverage |
|---------------|---------------------|-------|----------|
| [Team Obj A] | [Individual Obj 1] | [Name] | Covered |
| [Team Obj A] | [Individual Obj 2] | [Name] | Covered |
| [Team Obj B] | — | — | **Uncovered** |

### Issues

- [Same categories as above]

---

## Recommendations

- [Specific suggestions to improve alignment]
```

## Individual OKR Template

When writing individual-level OKRs, use a slightly adapted format:

```markdown
# [Name] — Individual OKRs — [Quarter]

**Role:** [Title]
**Team:** [Team name]
**Manager:** [Manager name]

---

## Objective 1: [Title]

> [Why this matters for the team and for your growth]

| # | Key Result | Baseline | Target | Supports team OKR |
|---|-----------|----------|--------|--------------------|
| 1.1 | [KR] | [Current] | [Target] | [Team Obj + KR #] |
| 1.2 | [KR] | [Current] | [Target] | [Team Obj + KR #] |

---

[Repeat for 2-3 objectives. Individuals should typically have fewer objectives than teams.]
```
